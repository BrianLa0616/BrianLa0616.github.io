Hop3Box Charity Youth Orchestra

At Hopebox Benefit Concerts, I use music to help raise money for disadvantaged children throughout the world and increase public awareness of the conditions of children. We use the funds to provide essential items the children need to survive. As the principal violist, I guided other students each week during sectionals even during quarantine.

Here are a list of the contributions made in the years that I have been involved:

  • 2020: HW House & Sunday Friends, have not yet counted
  • 2019: Sunday Friends & Guiding Light Project, donated $10,500
  • 2018: UNICEF for refugee children from Myanmar, donated $8,200
  • 2017: UNICEF for children in Haiti, donated $9,800

Senior Center Performances

With an ensemble of high school musicans, I perform several pieces at senior centers each month. Our repertoire range from classical music such as Shostakovich Valse No. 2 to recent pop-culture music such as the sountrack from Up.

Here are some of the senior centers I visited:

  • Saratoga Senior Center
  • Carlton Senior Living
  • Campbell Village
  • Westgate Villa

Street Performance

Each summer, I busk at Palo Alto Music Festival. In addition to playing pop music as an ensemble, I also perform solo music I have been working on. In past years, we raised and donated a total of $500 to Meals on Wheels America.



MUSE Benefit Concerts
×

MUSE Benefit Concerts

With an ensemble of high-school musicians, I perform classical and pop-culture music. This was my introduction to playing difficult classical quartet pieces such as Grieg Quartet No. 1, Op. 27. Using the money we raise, we support local organizations. In the past years, we have donated a total of $4000 to California Community Foundaition to fight the constant wildfires in California.

AMASE

I teach viola to children and adults on the autism spectrum and perform in concerts twice a year, supporting an area where people with disabilities can freely express themselves without prejudice. As a student leader, I provide advice to the AMASE coordinator to improve classes.

Taekwondo Instructor

At USA Taekwondo Academy, I lead exercises and teach sparring and poomsae to lower belts as a 3rd degree black belt and assistant master, fixing any minute flaws they might have in their stance or hand position.

Coding Instructor

With 2 of my friends, I started a programming class at Sedgwick Elementary School, teaching basic programming concepts to beginners using Scratch and Python. At the end of the course, they were able to create a simple game such as Guess the Number and Dr. Nim.

Academic Center Tutor

At Homestead High School Academic Center, I teach math and computer science to struggling underclassmen, clarifying any questions they might have and deepening their understanding of the subject.
