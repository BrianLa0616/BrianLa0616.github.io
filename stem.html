Réserve

Inspired by the toilet paper shortage that occured in the beginning of quaratine, my sister and I designed and developed an app that uses machine learning to give users a realistic approximation for how long their household supply will last. We calcualted this based on the number of people living in the house, average use rate of the general population, in addition to the use rate of the specific user after some data collection. Our hope is that with this tool, people will only purchase what they truly need. This application is still underdevelopment
